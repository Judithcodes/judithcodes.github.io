Hello, Welcome To RubiesBakes!

Here you will find: cakes,baking recipes, baking tips, food recipes and beautiful photographs of cakes and food.i have always find joy in the act and science of baking and preparing great meals and enjoy the end result of the sweet smell that fills the kitchen.

I have come to discover that there is great value in what I actually make and it connects me with people in special ways. So, after years of figuring out the best way to connect with more people outside my immediate environment, I came up with this. I am hoping you will find a good deal of baking inspiration and tips here, getting in the kitchen and baking is what it is all about.

About Me

Meet Olutobi Sodeinde

Hey there! I'm tobi. Thank you for stopping by. If you enjoy experimenting with cakes, you are in the right place.

I am a Nigerian banker, a graduate of business administration and an associate member of the Charted Institute of Personnel Management who loves to bake, cook and develop new recipes to share with friends and family. I grew up in a family where my mother is an exceptional cook and can convert the simplest of ingredients to a delicious meal and this help develop my passion for cooking and baking. This love has given birth to the need to start this site so I can share my experiences, success and failures in baking and cooking to teach and inspire others.

Hope you have a nice time browsing through.
